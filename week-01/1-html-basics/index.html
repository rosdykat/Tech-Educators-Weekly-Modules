woo cheesecake


I love cheesecakes. this is a picture of my cheesecake. this is what you need to make a cheesecake. enjoy!

Ingredients


  • 85g butter
  • 140g digestive biscuits
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 900g Philadelphia cheese
  • 250g golden caster sugar
  • 3 tbsp plain flour
  • 1 ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • finely grated zest of 1
  • 1 ½ tsp lemon juice
  • 3 large eggs
  • 284ml carton soured cream
  • Loads of blueberries

Author: Ross Fletcher
